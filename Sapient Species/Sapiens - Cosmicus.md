Neptune/Outer solar system

- Very tall but fragile frames (200+ cm) due to low gravity.
- Extremely pale, almost translucent skin; some veins visible.
- Large, luminous eyes with expanded irises, adapted for dim artificial light.
- Hair either absent or engineered for aesthetics, often silvery or white.
- Vitamin D synthesis built into their skin, giving them a faint bluish or pearlescent glow.
- Long fingers and fine motor coordination for machine work.
- A “ghostlike” appearance, elegant but uncanny.

Earthlings

**Highly Adaptable**
- Darker, UV-protective skin tones across most populations.
- More efficient water retention and tolerance to dehydration.
- Slimmer, heat-dissipating bodies (like tropical-adapted humans today).
- Possibly altered lungs and metabolism for fluctuating oxygen levels in megacities.
  
- - Average or even reduced height (160–170 cm), sturdy builds.
- Deeply tanned to charcoal-dark skin tones, thickened against UV radiation.
- Thickened brows and eyelids to shield eyes from glare.
- Wide chests and strong lung capacity for polluted or variable atmospheres.
- Coarse hair, often dark, sometimes patchy due to malnutrition.
- Weathered, rugged facial features; expressions appear harsher, more severe.
- Hands and feet calloused, built for manual labor and survival.

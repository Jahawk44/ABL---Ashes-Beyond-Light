Habitat: asteroid belt

- Shorter average height (140–160 cm) due to weak gravity.
- Slender, elongated limbs but reduced muscle mass.
- Larger eyes with reflective tapetum (like cats) for low-light environments.
- Pale to grayish skin, sometimes with dark freckling or pigmentation patches from radiation protection.
- Slightly hunched posture, adapted to crawling in tunnels.
- Fragile, birdlike bones with extensive medical/genetic reinforcement.
